My failures of

previous year

I’ve failed as an entrepreneur many times. I have found that being
open about my failures with family and friends helps lift the burden
and breathe new life into me. Ultimately, I have to make a decision
to let it go and stay focused on the present and future. It just
takes some time to process failure… kind of like the stages of
grief. Mourn the failure, then move on.

  • Not meeting others' expectations.
  • Missing a deadline.
  • Taking on too much/over-promising.
  • Failing an assignment.
  • Not getting a job.
  • Not getting into a club or making a team.
  • Losing a customer.
  • Staying in an unsatisfying job/major/class too long.
